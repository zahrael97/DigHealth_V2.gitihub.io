Moroccan Digital Health
Our vision is to innovate ans develop computational approaches that improve quality of healthcare in Morocco.

Research areas

Health Information Systems


Health Information Systems brings together the resources, tools and approaches needed to optimize the storage, retrieval and use of primary health care data and information. Health Information Systems tools may include clinical guidelines, formal medical terminology, and information and communication technologies.

Artificial intelligence


AI is indeed at the heart of the medicine of the future, with assisted operations, remote patient monitoring, and personalized treatments. Our team of researchers develop for this purpose multiple approaches and techniques, from language processing and ontologies construction, to data mining and machine learning

Bioinformatics and biostatistics

Analysis of therapeutic trials and epidemiological studies in different fields (cancer, cardiovascular diseases...), and biomarker identification associated with the diagnosis, prognosis, or therapeutic response. This is consistent with the integration of more data of modern biology such as the analysis of genome, proteome; identification of genetic markers of susceptibility, and sequencing of new generation DNA.
